Enchanting methods

There are four ways to enchant an item in Survival mode: 1. Through an enchanting table in exchange for experience points and lapis lazuli. Only unenchanted items may be enchanted this way. 2. Through an anvil, combining an enchanted book with an item. 3. Through an anvil, combining two of the same item with different existing enchantments into a single item that has the enchantments of both. 4. Through a librarian villager, which may enchant books respectively for emeralds instead of lapis lazuli and experience, which instead gives the player experience just like every trade.

"Enchanting is a mechanic that augments armor, tools, weapons, and books with a variety of enchantments that improve an item's existing abilities or imbue them with additional abilities and uses. A 'purple glint' animation appears on items to show that they are enchanted."
